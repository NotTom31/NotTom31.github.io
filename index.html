Design

Level design is something I've been passionate about since before I knew it was even a job. My journey in game making started early on by making adventure maps and constructing cities and castles in Minecraft. I discovered my passion for creating games in high school, where I made games for Arduinos, Construct engine. Throughout college, I learned to work with Unity and Unreal's terrain tools and UI systems.
